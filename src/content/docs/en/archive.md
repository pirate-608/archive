---
title: About the Archive
description: The entrance and organizing principle of Rain Altar Archive.
---

For now, this archive is divided into three loose kinds of material: works, notes, and resources.

Works hold fiction, settings, characters, and unfinished narrative worlds. Notes hold questions, sentences, images, and stray thoughts that have not yet found a more permanent home. Resources are reserved for maps, images, indexes, and fragments that may become useful later.

The site will stay plain for a while. It is not trying to look like a finished building. It is closer to an open desk: paper, marks, blank space, and ink still drying.
